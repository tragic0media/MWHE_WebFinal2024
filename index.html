Overview

Orange Collectivo was born out of the desire to create a space where creativity had no limits, a place where ideas could germinate and grow organically, like an orange growing at its own pace. It was conceived by a group of emerging artists who, like the fruit that inspires our brand, shared a fresh and vibrant vision of art and visual culture.

About us

At Orange Collective we like to think of each project as an orange: full of possibilities to be explored, with a rind that protects what is valuable and an interior full of creativity waiting to be squeezed. We are a collective of artists, designers, filmmakers and programmers who seek, like the orange, to surprise, refresh and revitalise. Each piece we create is intended to offer something unique, a visual experience that awakens the senses and leaves a lasting impression.

We specialise in fresh, vibrant and daring projects, always with the intention of breaking barriers and offering new perspectives. Like the orange in its nature, we believe in the power of the organic and the authentic. We are not afraid of criticism or experimentation; we know that true art is born when you take risks and challenge pre-established limits.
